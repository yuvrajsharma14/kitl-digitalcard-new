# Business Requirements Document (BRD)
# My Digital Card

**Version:** 1.1  
**Date:** 2026-04-21  
**Owner:** Yuvraj Sharma  
**Status:** In Development — Phase 1 Web nearly complete

---

## 1. Executive Summary

My Digital Card is a free web and mobile application that enables users to create, manage, and share professional digital business cards. The product eliminates the need for traditional physical business cards by providing a modern, eco-friendly, and always-accessible digital alternative.

---

## 2. Business Objectives

| # | Objective |
|---|---|
| 1 | Launch a free-to-use digital business card platform accessible via web and mobile |
| 2 | Enable users to create and share professional profiles instantly |
| 3 | Build a user base through organic sharing (every shared card is a product touchpoint) |
| 4 | Establish a scalable platform that can support future premium features |

---

## 3. Problem Statement

Professionals today still rely on physical business cards which are:
- Easy to lose or forget
- Expensive to print and reprint on every update
- Not eco-friendly
- Impossible to update after printing
- Hard to track engagement

**My Digital Card** solves this by providing a living, shareable, always-up-to-date digital identity card.
